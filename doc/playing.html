Flight 2942 Documentation

Requirements:

  • Love2d 0.7.2 [Linux|OS X|Windows]
  • Internet Connection

Controls

  • Space - Select Menu Item | Shoot
  • Up | Down - Change Menu Item Selection
  • Left | Right - Move Ship Left or Right
  • ESC - Return to menu, or if in menu, select exit|back.

Gameplay

Get your friends together and defend the galaxy!

When you log onto the server, a welcome message and your UID is printed to the console.

The amount of health (bacon) in the upper left represents your health. Get hit six times, and you die.

The left number displayed under health is your points.

The right number displayed under health is your team's points.

The higher the total points more enemies will come and they will speed up.

When you die, your high-score is printed to the console.
